Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining. Still have questions? WhatsApp us anytime.

Is Starhela legitimate and safe?
Yes. Starhela is a registered digital earning platform that has paid out millions to users across Africa. Payments are processed through official mobile money channels (M-Pesa, Airtel, MTN) and bank transfers. We prioritize user security with encrypted data and secure payment processing. Our team is available for support via WhatsApp and email to assist with any concerns. Hundreds of users have shared their positive experiences online, and we maintain a transparent track record of transactions.
What is the activation fee for?
The one-time activation fee (KSH 550 in Kenya) unlocks your full account access — all 12+ earning activities, instant withdrawals, and the referral program. You pay once and the account is yours forever. There are no monthly fees or hidden charges.
How and when do I get paid?
Earnings are credited to your Starhela wallet instantly after completing tasks. You can request a withdrawal at any time — minimum withdrawal amounts apply per payment method. Withdrawals are processed within minutes to hours via M-Pesa, Airtel Money, MTN Mobile Money, and bank transfer.
Can I earn from referrals too?
Yes! Once you have an active Starhela account, you get your own referral link. Earn $5 for every person you refer who joins and activates their account. Many top earners make a significant portion of their income through referrals.
Do I need any special skills or equipment?
No special skills are needed for most tasks — just a smartphone and internet connection. Tasks like watching videos, chatting, and clicking ads are available to everyone. Higher-paying tasks like video editing or AI labeling may require some basic skills but come with guides to help you get started.
