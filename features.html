Starhela FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Starhela before registering. Still have questions? WhatsApp us anytime.

Is Starhela legit? Is it a real earning platform?
Yes — Starhela is a registered digital earning platform that has paid out to thousands of users across Africa including Kenya, Uganda, Nigeria, and Tanzania. Payments are processed through official channels including M-Pesa, Airtel Money, MTN, and bank transfer. Our team is available via WhatsApp and email for support.
How much does Starhela registration cost in Kenya?
Starhela registration is free. There is a one-time account activation fee of KSH 550 in Kenya. This unlocks your full account — all 12+ earning activities, instant M-Pesa withdrawals, and the referral bonus program. You pay once and the account is yours for life. No monthly fees or hidden charges.
How do I withdraw my Starhela earnings via M-Pesa?
Earnings are credited to your Starhela wallet as you complete tasks. You can request a withdrawal at any time — minimum withdrawal amounts apply per payment method. Withdrawals are processed within minutes to hours via M-Pesa, Airtel Money, MTN Mobile Money, and bank transfer.
Can I earn from Starhela referrals?
Yes! Once your Starhela account is active, you get a personal referral link. Earn $5 for every person you refer who joins and activates their account. Many top Starhela earners across Kenya and Africa grow their income significantly through the referral program. No limit on referrals.
Do I need any special skills to earn on Starhela?
No special skills are required for most Starhela tasks — just a smartphone and internet connection. Tasks like watching videos, chatting with foreigners, and clicking ads are available to complete beginners. Higher-paying tasks like AI data labeling may require basic skills, but Starhela provides guides to help you get started quickly.
How much can I earn per month on Starhela?
Earnings depend on your activity level and which tasks you focus on. Active Starhela members in Kenya earn between KSH 50,000 and KSH 105,000 per month. The chat task is the highest paying, with top earners making up to $20.0 per day. The more tasks you complete, the more you earn.
