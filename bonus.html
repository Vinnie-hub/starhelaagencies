Agent Success Tips

How Top Agents Build Their Network

Tactics used by the highest-earning Starhela Agents across Africa.

💬
Lead with the Chat Earnings
When introducing Starhela to others, always start with the core earning method — chatting with foreigners. The Agent bonus is a bonus on top, not the headline.
🎯
Target People Looking for Income
Focus on students, stay-at-home parents, and people working from home. They're most likely to activate and become active — which is what earns your bonus.
📱
Use WhatsApp Groups & Channels
Share your referral link in community groups, university chats, and neighbourhood WhatsApp groups. A single active group can generate dozens of Level 1 activations.
🏫
Train Your Level 1 to Also Be Agents
The fastest way to grow your Level 2 and Level 3 income is to guide your Level 1 members to also introduce others. Their success becomes your passive income.
📊
Track Your Network Dashboard
Starhela's dashboard shows your full network depth, activation status, and bonus history. Check it weekly to see which level is growing fastest.
🤝
Support Your Referrals Early
Members who get support in their first week are far more likely to stay active. An active Level 1 network keeps your Level 2 and Level 3 bonuses flowing.
