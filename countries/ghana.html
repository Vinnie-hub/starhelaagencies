Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about chatting, activation, earnings, and Mobile Money withdrawals in Ghana.

How exactly does the chatting work? +
After activating your account, you'll be matched with international users from the USA, UK, Canada, and more who want friendly text conversations. You chat about your life in Ghana, local culture, travel destinations, food like jollof rice, Afrobeats music — anything naturally interesting — and earn 5–15 GHS per session. It's all text, no cameras or voice calls required. Chat as long as you like.
What is the 95 GHS activation fee for? +
The one-time 95 GHS payment unlocks your full account: access to chat matching with international users, all micro-task activities (surveys, AI labeling, video watching), and the ability to withdraw your earnings. There are no monthly fees ever. Most active chatters earn this back within their first 1–2 days of chatting.
How fast can I withdraw after activation? +
Once your wallet reaches 100 GHS — which typically takes 1–2 days of active chatting — you can withdraw instantly to MTN Mobile Money, Vodafone Cash, or AirtelTigo. Funds arrive in under 2 minutes. There's no waiting period or manual review required.
Do I need a computer? +
No! Starhela works perfectly on any Android or iPhone via your mobile browser. No app download is required. You can chat and earn from anywhere in Ghana using mobile data or WiFi — from home, at the market, between classes, anywhere.
Is the chatting safe and appropriate? +
Yes. All conversations are monitored by Starhela and must follow community guidelines — friendly, cultural, respectful topics only. Zero inappropriate content is tolerated. You chat under a screen name for privacy and can end any conversation at any time without any consequences to your account.
How many hours do I need to chat to earn well? +
Earnings scale with your activity. Casual chatters (1–2 hrs/day) typically earn 40–60 GHS. Active chatters (3–4 hrs/day) regularly earn 80–120 GHS. Top earners chatting 5+ hours make 200–500 GHS or more daily. You set your own hours and pace — there are no minimums.
What do I chat about with foreigners? +
International users are mainly curious about Ghanaian culture, food (jollof rice, fufu, waakye), travel (Cape Coast, Kakum, Accra), music (Afrobeats, highlife), festivals (Homowo, Panafest), languages, and daily life. You don't need to be an expert — just be yourself, share your genuine experiences, and ask questions back. Natural conversation earns the most.
Can I earn from other activities besides chatting? +
Yes! Chatting is your primary income, but you can also earn from paid surveys ($2–$5 each), AI data labeling tasks (up to $50/day), watching short videos ($0.60–$0.80 each), blogging, clicking ads, and referring friends for passive commissions.
