Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about chatting, activation, earnings and Mobile Money withdrawals.

How exactly does the chatting work? +
After activating your account, you'll be matched with international users (from USA, UK, Canada etc.) who want friendly text conversations. You chat with them — about your life in Ghana, culture, travel, food, anything — and earn 5–15 GHS per session. It's all text, no cameras or voice calls needed. You keep chatting for as long as you like.
What is the 95 GHS activation fee for? +
One-time payment unlocks your full account: access to chat matching, micro tasks, and withdrawal ability. No monthly fees — ever. Most active chatters earn this back within the first 1–2 days of chatting.
How fast can I withdraw after activation? +
Once your wallet reaches 100 GHS (usually within 1–2 days of active chatting), you can withdraw instantly to MTN, Vodafone Cash or AirtelTigo. Funds arrive in under 2 minutes.
Do I need a computer? +
No! StarHela works perfectly on any Android or iPhone via browser. No app download required. You can chat and earn from anywhere in Ghana using only mobile data or WiFi.
Is the chatting safe and appropriate? +
Yes. All conversations are monitored and must follow community guidelines — friendly, cultural, respectful topics only. Zero inappropriate content is tolerated. You chat under a screen name for privacy and can end any conversation at any time.
How many hours do I need to chat to earn well? +
It depends on your activity level. Casual chatters (1–2 hrs/day) typically earn 40–60 GHS. Active chatters (3–4 hrs/day) regularly earn 80–120 GHS. Top earners chatting 5+ hours make 150 GHS or more daily. You set your own hours.
What do I chat about with foreigners? +
International users are mainly curious about Ghanaian culture, food, travel destinations, music, festivals, daily life, and language. Topics like jollof rice, Accra, Kumasi, Afrobeats, Cape Coast, and Ghanaian traditions are very popular. You don't need to be an expert — just be yourself and share your experiences naturally.
