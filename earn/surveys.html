FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How much can I realistically earn?
Most active members earn $15–$60 per week. Members with complete profiles and strong match rates consistently earn $100–$300 per month. Results vary based on survey availability, your profile completeness, and how much time you invest. These are real member averages, not guarantees.
How quickly do I get paid?
Survey rewards are credited to your Starhela wallet within 24 hours of approval. Mobile money withdrawals are typically processed within a few hours on business days. Minimum withdrawal amounts apply.
What countries can participate?
Starhela is available across 30+ African countries including Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, Uganda, Tanzania, Ethiopia, South Africa, Zambia, Rwanda, Senegal, Ivory Coast, Cameroon, and more. If your country has mobile money services, you can participate.
Do I need any experience or qualifications?
None at all. Anyone aged 18+ with a smartphone and internet access can participate. No special education or prior experience is required. A more complete profile means more and better-paying survey matches.
Is my personal data safe?
Yes. We use encryption to protect all your data. Your personal information is never sold to third parties. Only anonymised, aggregated survey responses are shared with research clients. Full details in our privacy policy.
Why do surveys expire or fill up?
Research clients need a specific number of responses from targeted demographics. Once the quota is filled, that survey closes. This is why completing your profile increases your earning potential — you get matched faster to relevant surveys before they fill up.
