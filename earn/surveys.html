Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How much can I realistically earn from surveys?
Earnings depend on how many surveys you complete, your profile match rate, and which categories you qualify for. Most active members earn $15–$60 per week. Dedicated members with complete profiles and high match rates earn $100–$300 per month. Results vary and are not guaranteed.
How quickly are payments processed?
Most survey payments are credited to your Starhela wallet within 24 hours of survey approval. M-Pesa withdrawals are typically processed within a few hours during business days. Minimum withdrawal amounts may apply.
What types of surveys are available?
We offer surveys across consumer products, technology, health & wellness, automotive, finance, food & beverage, entertainment and market research. New surveys from global brands are added daily. Survey availability depends on your profile and demographics.
Do I need any special qualifications?
None at all. Anyone aged 18+ with a smartphone and internet access can participate. No special education or experience is required. The more complete your profile, the more surveys you'll qualify for.
Why is there a $ 550 registration fee?
The one-time $ 550 activation fee covers your lifetime platform access, onboarding training, and account verification. There are no monthly fees, no hidden charges, and no recurring payments. This fee helps us maintain a quality community of serious earners.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes. We use encryption to protect your data. Your personal information is never sold to third parties. Only anonymised, aggregated survey responses are shared with research clients. You can review our privacy policy for full details.
